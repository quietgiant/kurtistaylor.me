Current Project: Automated Trading Systems

The stock market was fascinating to watch in 2020. During that year, I started developing automated trading systems that utilize innovative technologies and sophisticated finanical strategies in order to find an edge in the stock market. The modules in the systems are primarily written in C# and Python, and use a variety of fun and experimental new technologies. To execute orders, the systems mostly use Alpaca, but I also experiment with other brokers. See my stocks page for more info.

Modeling the Spread of Influence in Social Networks

A research project I assisted Dr. Zesheng Chen with during Spring 2016, which explores traditional methods to model the spread of influence in social networks and proposes a new, more accurate model. Our findings can help computer scientists in designing new network protocols, structures, and policies to facilitate the spread of information through social networks. This paper was published in the 2017 IEEE 37th International Conference on Distributed Computing Systems Workshops.
